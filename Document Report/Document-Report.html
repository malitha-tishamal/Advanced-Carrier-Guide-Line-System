Advanced Career Guideline System

Role-Based Intelligent Career Management Platform for SLIATE

📌 Introduction

The Advanced Career Guideline System is a centralized web-based platform developed for SLIATE to support students, alumni, lecturers, administrators, and companies through intelligent career guidance, professional profiling, and structured talent discovery.

🎯 System Objectives

  • Centralized career management system
  • Professional digital profiles for students
  • Skill-based student discovery
  • Industry-oriented talent matching
  • Modern dashboards for academic administration

👥 User Roles

Admin
Lecturer
Active Student
Former Student
Company

🔐 Admin Module

  • Dashboard with login analytics
  • Admin, Lecturer, Student & Company management
  • Approve / Disable / Edit / Delete accounts
  • Skill management (2700+ skills, 13+ categories)
  • Reports & feedback handling
  • Course and curriculum management

🧑‍🏫 Lecturer Module

  • Monitor student & alumni profiles
  • Approve students
  • View skills, education & achievements
  • Support academic guidance

🎓 Student & Alumni Module

  • LinkedIn-style professional profile
  • Achievements & Certifications
  • Education & Work Experience timeline
  • Skills & Projects (max 20 skills)
  • Smart ranking visibility

🏢 Company Module

  • Top candidate recommendations
  • Match quality scoring
  • Advanced skill-based filters
  • Active & former student discovery

🧠 Intelligent Matching Logic

The system applies an intelligent rule-based ranking mechanism considering:

  • Skills (highest priority)
  • Projects
  • Certifications
  • Education
  • Work experience
  • Profile activity

🚀 System Impact

This platform bridges the gap between education and employment by enabling students to showcase verified skills and achievements while allowing companies to efficiently identify suitable candidates.
